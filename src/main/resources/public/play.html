APEX PAC-MAN GAME - Play

How to Play:

1. Use "↑, ↓, ←, →" on your keyboard, feel free to play.
2. Press "Start/Re-Start Game" to start the game.
3. Press "Back to Homepage" to go back to the homepage to select level.
4. Get 2400 points to win the game. Losing 3 lives will result in a game over.
